# JLPT N2 漢字 — Kanji Learning Guide

> **Level:** Upper Intermediate | **Prerequisite:** N5 + N4 + N3 mastery
>
> N2 kanji unlock newspaper articles, business Japanese, and nuanced expression. These 66 kanji are organized by theme to build contextual reading power.

---

## How to Use This Guide

1. **Work through one section per study session.** Each section is themed so vocabulary reinforces naturally.
2. **Read example sentences aloud.** Context-based learning cements kanji faster than rote drilling.
3. **Test yourself with the review section** at the end after completing all 10 sections.
4. **Write each kanji by hand** — muscle memory is your strongest ally at N2 level.

### Study Tip

> **N2 kanji are about nuance.** At this level, you already know most radicals and basic meanings — the challenge is distinguishing similar kanji and reading them in context. The best strategy: **read native material daily** (NHK News Web Easy → NHK News Web, manga with furigana → without). When you encounter a kanji you studied here, mark it in your checklist above. Kanji truly "learned" are kanji you've met in the wild — not just in a study guide. 頑張って！
