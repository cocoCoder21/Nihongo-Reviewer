# JLPT N4 漢字 — Kanji Learning Guide

> **Level:** Upper Beginner | **Total Kanji:** ~170 | **Prerequisite:** N5 Kanji mastery
>
> N4 Kanji expand your ability to read everyday texts, simple news, and longer conversations. Many N4 Kanji build on radicals you already know from N5.

---

## How to Use This Guide

1. **Review N5 radicals first.** N4 Kanji heavily reuse 言, 人, 木, 手, 水, 心, 力.
2. **Study one section per session** (10–15 Kanji max).
3. **Write compound words** — most N4 Kanji appear in two-Kanji compounds (熟語).
4. **Read the example sentences aloud** to connect Kanji to grammar.

## Section 1: Strength & Movement — 力と動き

> **Radical in focus:** 力 (power/strength)
>
> The 力 radical appears in many action and effort Kanji.

---

### 力 — Power / Strength

- **Onyomi:** リョク・リキ | **Kunyomi:** ちから
- **Radical:** 力 (power)
- **Vocabulary:**
  - 力（ちから）— strength / power
  - 電力（でんりょく）— electric power
  - 能力（のうりょく）— ability
- **Example:** もっと力を入れてください。— Please put in more effort.
- **Mnemonic:** A flexed arm — raw muscular power.

---

### 動 — Move

- **Onyomi:** ドウ | **Kunyomi:** うご（く）
- **Radical:** 力 (power)
- **Vocabulary:**
  - 動く（うごく）— to move
  - 動物（どうぶつ）— animal
  - 自動車（じどうしゃ）— automobile
- **Example:** この機械は動きません。— This machine doesn't move.
- **Mnemonic:** Heavy things 重 need power 力 to move.

---

### 勉 — Exertion / Effort

- **Onyomi:** ベン | **Kunyomi:** —
- **Radical:** 力 (power)
- **Vocabulary:**
  - 勉強（べんきょう）— study
  - 勉強する（べんきょうする）— to study
  - 勉強家（べんきょうか）— studious person
- **Example:** 毎晩勉強しています。— I study every evening.
- **Mnemonic:** Giving birth 免 requires tremendous power 力 — study takes effort too.

---

### 強 — Strong

- **Onyomi:** キョウ・ゴウ | **Kunyomi:** つよ（い）
- **Radical:** 弓 (bow)
- **Vocabulary:**
  - 強い（つよい）— strong
  - 勉強（べんきょう）— study
  - 強力（きょうりょく）— powerful
- **Example:** 風が強いです。— The wind is strong.
- **Mnemonic:** A drawn bow 弓 with a bug 虫 — even a small insect pulling a bow is "strong."

---

### 弱 — Weak

- **Onyomi:** ジャク | **Kunyomi:** よわ（い）
- **Radical:** 弓 (bow)
- **Vocabulary:**
  - 弱い（よわい）— weak
  - 弱点（じゃくてん）— weak point
  - 弱虫（よわむし）— coward
- **Example:** 体が弱いです。— My body is weak.
- **Mnemonic:** Two bows 弓弓 decorated with ice ⺀ — ornamental bows can't fire, they're "weak."

---

### 働 — Work (labor)

- **Onyomi:** ドウ | **Kunyomi:** はたら（く）
- **Radical:** 亻 (person)
- **Vocabulary:**
  - 働く（はたらく）— to work
  - 労働（ろうどう）— labor
  - 働き者（はたらきもの）— hard worker
- **Example:** 父は銀行で働いています。— My father works at a bank.
- **Mnemonic:** A person 亻 using power 力 with heavy things 重 — a person laboring.

## Section 3: Speech & Communication — 言葉と伝達
