## Grammar Points

### 1. ここ／そこ／あそこ は N です。

- Same こ/そ/あ system as Lesson 2 but for **places**.
- **ここ** — here (near speaker)
- **そこ** — there (near listener)
- **あそこ** — over there (far from both)

**Examples:**
- ここは 食堂です。 — This place is the cafeteria.
- そこは 教室です。 — That place is the classroom.
- トイレは あそこです。 — The toilet is over there.

---

### 2. N は place です。(Location of a thing/place)

- To say where something is:

**Examples:**
- トイレは どこですか。…あそこです。 — Where is the toilet? …Over there.
- 電話は 1階です。 — The telephone is on the 1st floor.
- ミラーさんの 会社は どこですか。 — Where is Mr. Miller's company?

---

### 3. こちら／そちら／あちら／どちら

- Polite versions of ここ/そこ/あそこ/どこ.
- Also means "this direction / that direction."

**Examples:**
- お国は どちらですか。 — Where are you from? (polite)
- 会議室は こちらです。 — The conference room is this way.

---

### 4. N1 の N2 (location + specific place)

- の used to specify which branch or affiliate.

**Examples:**
- これは どこの 靴ですか。…イタリアの 靴です。 — Where are these shoes from? …Italian shoes.

## Mini Quiz

**Q1.** Ask "Where is the library?" politely.

**Q2.** Answer "The reception is on the 2nd floor."

**Q3.** Someone asks お国は どちらですか。 You're from America. Answer politely.

**Q4.** Say "Here is the conference room."

**Q5.** Ask "What floor is the restaurant on?"
