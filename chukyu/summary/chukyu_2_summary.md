## 付録 B — 類似文法比較 Similar Grammar Comparison Tables

### Certainty / Emphasis Spectrum

| Expression | Nuance | Strength |
|------------|--------|----------|
| ～に過ぎない | Merely; nothing more than (minimizing) | ▼ Low |
| ～とも言える | You could also say that | ■ Neutral |
| ～に他ならない | Nothing other than (=precisely this) | ▲ High |
| ～にほかならない | None other than (=precisely this) | ▲ High |
| ～と言っても過言ではない | No exaggeration to say | ▲▲ Very High |
| ～こそ | It IS precisely X | ▲▲ Very High |
| ～ばこそ | PRECISELY because X | ▲▲ Very High |

### Concession / Contrast

| Expression | Nuance | Register |
|------------|--------|----------|
| ～けれども / けど | Simple contrast | Casual–Polite |
| ～ものの | Although (concessive, written) | Written/Formal |

| ～にしても | Even granting that / even if | All |
| ～反面 | While X is true, the flip side… | Written/Formal |
| ～からといって | Just because X ≠ Y | All |

| Expression | Strength | Register |
|------------|----------|----------|

### Keigo Register Reminder (L13)

| Context | Style |
|---------|-------|
| 社外（to outside company） | Use 謙譲語 for your company's people: 部長の田中は 席を外しております |
| 社内（within company） | Use 尊敬語 for superiors: 田中部長は 席を外していらっしゃいます |
| 身内（personal context） | Lower your family: 母は 出かけております |
