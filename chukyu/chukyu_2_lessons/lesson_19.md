## Grammar Points

### 1. ～かねない (Might ~ / There's a risk of ~)

**Meaning:** There's a risk that ~ might happen. (Negative/unwanted possibility.)

**Formation:** V ます-stem + かねない

**Examples:**
- フェイクニュースは 社会問題に なりかねない。 — Fake news could become a social problem.
- 情報を 確認しないと、誤解を 招きかねません。 — If you don't verify info, it might cause misunderstandings.

---

### 2. ～得る（うる）/ ～得ない（えない） (Can possibly ~ / Cannot possibly ~)

**Meaning:** It's possible/conceivable that ~ / It's impossible that ~.

**Formation:** V ます-stem + 得る / 得ない

**Examples:**
- そういうことは 十分 あり得ます。 — That's entirely possible.
- そんなことは あり得ない。 — That's impossible.
- 誰にでも 起こり得ることです。 — It's something that could happen to anyone.

---

### 3. ～に他ならない (Nothing other than ~ / Precisely ~)

**Meaning:** It is nothing other than ~ / Precisely because ~. Strong assertion.

**Formation:** N / Plain form + に他ならない

**Examples:**
- それは 偏見に他なりません。 — That's nothing other than prejudice.
- 彼が 成功したのは 努力の 結果に他ならない。 — His success is nothing other than the result of effort.

---

### 4. ～まい (Will probably not ~ / Determined not to ~)

**Meaning:** (a) Probably won't ~. (b) I'm determined not to ~. (Literary/formal negative volitional.)

**Formation:** V dictionary + まい (I-verbs) / V ます-stem + まい (II-verbs)

**Examples:**
- もう あんな 記事は 信じまい。 — I'll never believe such articles again.
- 彼は もう 来るまい。 — He probably won't come.

---

## Mini Quiz

**Q1.** Say "Spreading unverified info could cause problems" using ～かねない.

**Q2.** Express "Mistakes like that are entirely possible" using ～得る.

**Q3.** Say "The cause is nothing other than carelessness" using ～に他ならない.

**Q4.** Express "I will never be fooled by fake news again" using ～まい.

**Q5.** Give advice: "You shouldn't accept online information uncritically."
