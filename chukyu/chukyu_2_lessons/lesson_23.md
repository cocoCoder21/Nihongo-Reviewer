## Grammar Points

### 1. ～を背景に (Against the backdrop of ~)

**Meaning:** With ~ as the background / driven by ~.

**Formation:** N + を背景に / を背景にして

**Examples:**
- グローバル化を背景に、企業の 海外進出が 増えています。 — Against the backdrop of globalization, companies expanding overseas are increasing.
- 円安を背景に、輸出が 好調です。 — Driven by the weak yen, exports are doing well.

---

### 2. ～反面 / ～半面 (On the flip side / While ~)

**Meaning:** While ~ on one hand, on the other ~. Two-sided evaluation.

**Formation:** V plain / adj + 反面

**Examples:**
- グローバル化は 便利な反面、格差を 広げる 危険も あります。 — While globalization is convenient, there's also the danger of widening disparities.
- 円安は 輸出に いい反面、輸入コストが 上がります。 — While a weak yen is good for exports, import costs rise.

---

### 3. ～ないまでも (Even if not ~ / At least ~)

**Meaning:** Even if not ~ at least ~.

**Formation:** V ない + までも

**Examples:**
- 完全に 解決しないまでも、改善は 必要です。 — Even if we can't completely solve it, improvement is necessary.
- 専門家でないまでも、基本的な 知識は 持つべきです。 — Even if you're not an expert, you should have basic knowledge.

---

### 4. ～ばこそ (Precisely because ~)

**Meaning:** It is precisely because ~ that ~. (More literary/emphatic than からこそ.)

**Formation:** V ば + こそ

**Examples:**
- 困難が あればこそ、成長できるのです。 — It's precisely because there are difficulties that we can grow.
- 競争が あればこそ、技術が 進歩するのです。 — It's precisely because there's competition that technology advances.

## Mini Quiz

**Q1.** Say "Against the backdrop of AI development, many jobs are changing" using ～を背景に.

**Q2.** Express "The internet is convenient, but on the flip side, privacy is at risk" using ～反面.

**Q3.** Say "Even if you can't invest a lot, you should save some" using ～ないまでも.

**Q4.** Express "Precisely because there are challenges, innovation happens" using ～ばこそ.

**Q5.** Describe: "Japan's economy faces both opportunities and challenges from globalization."
