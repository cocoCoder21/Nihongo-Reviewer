## Grammar Points

### 1. ～に応じて (According to ~ / In response to ~)

**Meaning:** Depending on ~ / In accordance with ~. Adjustment to a variable.

**Formation:** N + に応じて / に応じた N

**Examples:**
- 能力に応じて 給料が 決まります。 — Salary is determined according to ability.
- 状況に応じて 柔軟に 対応します。 — We respond flexibly according to the situation.
- 経験に応じた ポジションを 用意しています。 — We have positions according to experience.

---

### 2. ～に限らず (Not limited to ~)

**Meaning:** Not only ~ / Not limited to ~.

**Formation:** N + に限らず

**Examples:**
- 女性に限らず、男性も 育児休暇を 取るべきです。 — Not only women, but men should also take parental leave.
- 日本に限らず、多くの 国で 高齢化が 進んでいます。 — Not limited to Japan, aging is advancing in many countries.

---

### 3. ～からといって (Just because ~ doesn't mean)

**Meaning:** Just because ~ (doesn't mean ~). Warns against hasty conclusions.

**Formation:** Plain form + からといって ～わけではない / ～とは限らない

**Examples:**
- 残業が 多いからといって、仕事が できるとは限りません。 — Just because someone works overtime a lot doesn't mean they're capable.
- 給料が 高いからといって、幸せなわけではありません。 — Just because salary is high doesn't mean you're happy.

---

### 4. ～ものの (Although ~ / Even though ~)

**Meaning:** Although ~. Concession — written/formal register.

**Formation:** V plain / adj + ものの

**Examples:**
- テレワークを 導入したものの、うまく いかない 会社も あります。 — Although telework was introduced, some companies aren't managing well.
- 転職を 考えているものの、なかなか 決められません。 — Although I'm thinking of changing jobs, I can't quite decide.

## Mini Quiz

**Q1.** Say "Working hours are adjusted according to family situation" using ～に応じて.

**Q2.** Express "Not limited to young people, older workers also want flexibility" using ～に限らず.

**Q3.** Say "Just because you have free time doesn't mean life is fulfilling" using ～からといって.

**Q4.** Express "Although I took paid leave, I couldn't fully rest" using ～ものの.

**Q5.** Describe: "It's important to balance work and personal life."
