## Grammar Points

### 1. ～に伴って / ～に伴い (Along with ~ / As ~ happens)

**Meaning:** As ~ progresses, ~ also changes. Parallel change.

**Formation:** N / V plain + に伴って / に伴い

**Examples:**
- IT化に伴って、働き方も 変わってきました。 — Along with IT adoption, work styles have also changed.
- 人口の 増加に伴い、環境問題が 深刻に なっています。 — Along with population growth, environmental problems are becoming serious.

---

### 2. ～にかけて (Over the period of ~ / Extending to)

**Meaning:** From ~ to ~ (time or space range, not precise endpoints).

**Formation:** N から N にかけて

**Examples:**
- 1990年代から 2000年代にかけて、インターネットが 急速に 普及しました。 — From the 1990s to 2000s, the internet spread rapidly.
- 関東から 東北にかけて 大雪が 降るでしょう。 — Heavy snow is expected from Kanto through Tohoku.

---

### 3. ～ばかりか ～も (Not only ~ but also ~)

**Meaning:** Not only ~ but also ~. Stronger than だけでなく.

**Formation:** N / V plain + ばかりか ～ も

**Examples:**
- スマホは 若者ばかりか、お年寄りも 使っています。 — Not only young people but also the elderly use smartphones.
- 仕事が 増えたばかりか、給料も 下がりました。 — Not only did work increase, but salary also went down.

---

### 4. ～つつある (In the process of ~ / gradually ~)

**Meaning:** Is gradually ~-ing. (Written, formal — ongoing change.)

**Formation:** V ます-stem + つつある

**Examples:**
- AIの 技術は 急速に 進歩しつつあります。 — AI technology is rapidly advancing.
- 現金を 使わない 生活が 広がりつつあります。 — A cashless lifestyle is gradually spreading.

## Mini Quiz

**Q1.** Say "Along with technological progress, life has become more convenient" using ～に伴って.

**Q2.** Express "From spring to summer, it will get hotter" using ～にかけて.

**Q3.** Say "Not only students but also adults are studying programming" using ～ばかりか.

**Q4.** Express "The number of remote workers is gradually increasing" using ～つつある.

**Q5.** Describe a trend: "Compared to 10 years ago, AI use has increased significantly."
