## Grammar Points

### 1. ～に限り / ～に限って (Limited to ~ / Only ~)

**Meaning:** Limited to ~ / Only for ~. Also: ～に限って = only in the case of ~ (often with ironic nuance).

**Formation:** N + に限り / に限って

**Examples:**
- この 割引は 学生に限ります。 — This discount is limited to students.
- 今日に限って 電車が 遅れた。 — Of all days, the train was late today.
- 先着 20名に限り 無料です。 — It's free, limited to the first 20 people.

---

### 2. ～を問わず (Regardless of ~)

**Meaning:** Regardless of ~ / irrespective of ~.

**Formation:** N + を問わず

**Examples:**
- 年齢を問わず、誰でも 参加できます。 — Regardless of age, anyone can participate.
- 天候を問わず、イベントは 行います。 — The event will proceed regardless of weather.
- 経験を問わず 募集しています。 — We're hiring regardless of experience.

---

### 3. ～に基づいて (Based on ~)

**Meaning:** Based on ~ / on the basis of ~.

**Formation:** N + に基づいて / に基づき / に基づいた N

**Examples:**
- 調査に基づいて 計画を 立てました。 — We made a plan based on research.
- 事実に基づいた 映画です。 — It's a movie based on true events.

---

### 4. ～次第 (As soon as ~ / Depends on ~)

**Meaning:** (a) As soon as ~ (after verb). (b) Depends on ~ (after noun).

**Formation:** (a) V ます-stem + 次第 (b) N + 次第

**Examples:**
- 届き次第、連絡します。 — I'll contact you as soon as it arrives.
- 成功するかどうかは 努力次第です。 — Whether you succeed depends on effort.
- 天気次第で 予定が 変わります。 — Plans change depending on the weather.

## Mini Quiz

**Q1.** Say "This offer is limited to members only" using ～に限り.

**Q2.** Express "Regardless of nationality, everyone can apply" using ～を問わず.

**Q3.** Say "We'll decide based on the schedule" using ～に基づいて.

**Q4.** Say "I'll call you as soon as the reservation is confirmed" using ～次第.

**Q5.** Describe a trip plan: "I want to stay at a Japanese inn and visit famous spots."
